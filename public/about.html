Our Story

At Mini Miracles Child Development Rehabilitation Center, our journey began with a vision to provide exceptional care and support for children and families facing developmental challenges. Founded by Ms. Botta Ramya, a licensed Rehabilitation Psychologist, we are committed to delivering evidence-based psychological services, occupational therapy, speech therapy, special education, and more. Our aim is to empower children, adolescents, and adults to reach their full potential through comprehensive, compassionate, and personalized care.

Community Engagement

We believe in the importance of giving back to our community. Mini Miracles actively participates in outreach programs, health education, and awareness initiatives to help parents, caregivers, and children. We strive to make a positive impact beyond our center, supporting families and individuals in their journey toward better mental health and development.

FAQ

What services do you offer at Mini Miracles Child Development Rehabilitation Center?

We provide a range of specialized services, including psychological assessments, speech therapy, occupational therapy, behavioral therapy, cognitive rehabilitation, special education, and counseling services for children, adolescents, and adults.

Who can benefit from your services?

Our services are designed to support children and adults facing developmental, psychological, or behavioral challenges. We work with individuals diagnosed with autism, developmental delays, speech or language disorders, learning disabilities, and more.

How can I schedule an appointment with your specialists?

You can schedule an appointment by contacting our center directly through our website or by calling us. We will guide you through the process of booking an appointment with one of our experienced professionals.

What is your approach to therapy and treatment?

We use evidence-based therapies and a personalized approach to treatment. Our services are designed to meet the individual needs of each client, ensuring that they receive the most effective care for their specific developmental and psychological needs.
