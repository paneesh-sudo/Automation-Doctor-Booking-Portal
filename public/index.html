FAQ

What is a Child Development and Rehabilitation Center, and how does it differ from other healthcare facilities?

A Child Development and Rehabilitation Center specializes in providing a range of services aimed at supporting the growth, development, and well-being of children with developmental delays, disabilities, or special needs. Unlike general healthcare facilities, these centers focus on tailored therapies, early interventions, and holistic care to help children achieve their full potential.
